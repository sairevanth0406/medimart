Guides

Step-by-step instructions to help you use Medimart effectively

How to Place an Order
  1. Log in to your Medimart account or register if you're a new user.
  2. Browse the available medicines and add them to your cart.
  3. Proceed to checkout and enter your delivery details.
  4. Choose a payment method and complete your purchase.
  5. You'll receive a confirmation email with your order details.
How to Track Your Order
  1. Log in to your Medimart account.
  2. Go to the "Orders" section in your dashboard.
  3. Find the order you want to track and click "View Details".
  4. Use the tracking number provided to monitor your order's status.
How to Manage Your Account
  1. Log in to your Medimart account.
  2. Navigate to the "Account Settings" section.
  3. Update your personal information, delivery address, or payment methods.
  4. Save your changes to apply them.
How to Contact Support
  1. Visit the "Help Center" page.
  2. Click on the "Contact Support" button.
  3. Fill out the form with your query and submit it.
