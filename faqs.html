FAQs

Find answers to common questions about Medimart

How do I register as a customer?
To register as a customer, click on the "Register" link on the homepage, fill out the required details, and submit your information to create an account.
How can I log in to my account?
You can log in by clicking the "Login" link in the top navigation. Enter your registered email and password to access your account and view your order history.
How do I place an order for medicines?
To place an order, browse through our available medicines, add the desired items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Complete the payment to finalize the order.
Can I view my previous orders?
Yes, once logged in, you can access your order history from your account dashboard, where you can view details of past orders and track their status.
What should I do if I need help with an order?
If you need assistance with an order, you can reach our customer support team via the contact page or by emailing us at support@medimart.com.
How can I cancel my order?
Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of placing them. Simply go to your order history, select the order, and click the "Cancel" option if the order hasn't been processed for shipment yet.
What can I do if I am an employee?
Employees can log in to manage sales, update stock, and view customer orders. If you are a new employee, please contact your administrator to get access.
How do I add new medicines to the system?
Admins can add new medicines to the system by logging into the admin panel. Navigate to the "Manage Medicines" section, where you can add new items, including details like name, price, and stock.
How do I add a new employee to the system?
Admins can add new employees by logging into the admin panel and navigating to the "Manage Employees" section. From there, you can add their details and assign appropriate roles.
How can I manage suppliers?
Admins can manage suppliers by accessing the "Manage Suppliers" section in the admin panel. This allows you to view, add, or remove suppliers as needed.
